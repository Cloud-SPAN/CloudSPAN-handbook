1.1 About Cloud-SPAN

Cloud-SPAN deploys high quality learning resources that will train researchers to effectively generate and analyse a range of ’omics data using Cloud computing resources.

Cloud-SPAN is collaboration between Department of Biology at the University of York and the Software Sustainability Institute funded by the UKRI innovation scholars award. Project Reference: MR/V038680/1
