“The goal of this document is to create a contribution process that: - Encourages new contributions. - Encourages contributors to remain involved. - Avoids unnecessary processes and bureaucracy whenever possible. - Creates a transparent decision making process which makes it clear how contributors can be involved in decision making.”

“Creating barriers to entry just reduces the number of people there’s a chance to identify, educate and potentially grow into greater contributors.”
